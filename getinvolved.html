Get Involved

Join Our Team

Do you love tech and want to get more involved within the Sudo community?

We’re always looking for passionate and innovative individuals to support and work alongside our team. On our team, you can assist with creating workshop content, marketing Sudo to the community, or even planning the monthly events. Programming experience isn’t required, but curiosity is an asset!

To apply, send your resume and a short paragraph about yourself to Melissa at melissa@sudolabs.ca.

Volunteer with Us

All of our workshops are possible because of our amazing volunteers.

If you have experience and are passionate about programming, come help us build our community by sharing what you know with the Kingston community. At each event we have one volunteer instructor to teach and inspire the participants, and a group of volunteer mentors to support the instructor and offer any assistance to participants in need of help.

To apply to either volunteer position, send your resume and a short paragraph about yourself to hello@sudolabs.ca.

Donate

At Sudo, we aim to improve digital literacy in Kingston.

We become closer to our goals by offering free coding workshops, hosting meet-ups at local coffee shops, and connecting women interested in tech with experienced developers.

All of our events are free of charge, and we honour ourselves in being a dedicated and accessible resource to all interested in coding. Your donation will help us train and inspire more women into becoming great developers.

You can support our cause by donating here, or if you’re a business and interested in sponsorship, please email us at sponsorship@sudolabs.ca.
